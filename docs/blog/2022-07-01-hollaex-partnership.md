# OctoBot is now supporting Hollaex

![cover](/images/blog/hollaex-partnership/cover.jpg)

OctoBot is excited to announce that it has joined Hollaex, the world’s first decentralized cryptocurrency exchange platform, in its list of partner trading bots!

<!--truncate-->

## What is Hollaex

<a href="https://www.hollaex.com?utm_source=octobot" rel="nofollow">HollaEx</a> is more than just a white-label crypto software, it's
your go to tool kit that connects your business to the blockchain world. HollaEx
let's you start an exchange, with your own markets and assets on your domain.
Hollaex is an open source exchange software kit that allows anyone to start a
crypto business. OctoBot is now the first crypto trading bot to integrate
Hollaex support!

## How to use Hollaex in OctoBot

OctoBot supports Hollaex trading through a dedicated module. A simple configuration guide is available at on [the HollaEx account setup](/guides/exchanges/hollaex/account-setup).
