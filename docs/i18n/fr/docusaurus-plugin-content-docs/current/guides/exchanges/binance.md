## Détails du partneriat

Binance has 2 requirements for an account to support OctoBot:

- A relatively new account \(after july 1st 2021\)
- 528112221 as a referral id (or no referral id) on the account

In case your current account is not meeting these criteria, you can support OctoBot by creating a new account on <a href="https://accounts.binance.com/en/register?ref=528112221" rel="nofollow">accounts.binance.com/en/register?ref=528112221</a>.

Please note that thanks to <a href="https://www.binance.com/en-NG/support/faq/360037037312" rel="nofollow">Internal Transfers</a>, you can move your funds quickly and for free between Binance accounts.
